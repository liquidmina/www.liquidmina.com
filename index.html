How liquidMINA Works

One unified cryptographic pipeline. From MINA deposit to yield-bearing LST on Solana (and Ethereum in Phase 2). No Guardians. No trust assumptions. Pure ZK Math.

Step 01

Stake MINA in the Vault

You deposit native MINA into the o1js Mina Vault zkApp. The vault immediately delegates your stake to high-performing validators, beginning yield accrual. A ZK-proof of this lock event is generated on the Mina chain using Pickles recursive proofs.

Step 02

ZK-Prover Network Converts the Proof

A decentralized network of ZK-Provers picks up the Pickles proof and converts it into a Solana-optimized Groth16 proof. This step is permissionless — any prover can compete for the fee, ensuring liveness and decentralization at the proof-generation layer.

Pickles → Groth16 · Decentralized
Step 03

Solana Light Client Verifies

The Groth16 proof is submitted to our Anchor-based Solana Light Client — the cheapest ZK-verifier in the market thanks to Mina's 22KB state root. The program validates the proof against Mina's current state root. No verification = no bridge. Period.

Anchor Program · On-Chain Verification

Step 04

LST Minted to Your Wallet

Upon successful verification, the Bridge Program mints MINA LST (Liquid Stake Tokens) at a 1:1 ratio directly to your Solana wallet. Your LST is immediately composable — usable as collateral on Kamino, Jupiter, or any SPL-compatible DeFi protocol.

1:1 Peg · Instant Composability
Step 05

Yield Accrues — Automatically

The Solana Program continuously updates the InterestBearingConfig extension based on live Mina staking APY. Your MINA LST balance grows every epoch according to the compound interest formula — no claiming, no vaults, no extra gas.

[Three-Layer Architecture]

No Single Entity Controls Your Funds.

L1
Mina Vault · o1js
Stake native MINA. The protocol delegates to validators and generates Pickles recursive SNARK proofs of every deposit event. Immutable, non-custodial.
L2
ZK-Prover Network
Decentralized provers convert Mina's Pickles proofs to Solana-compatible Groth16 proofs. Permissionless participation with economic incentives for liveness. Mesa Upgrade optimized for succinctness.
L3
Solana Light Client · Anchor
Acts as the canonical "source of truth" on Solana. Verifies Groth16 proofs against Mina state roots. Controls the Token2022 mint authority — can only mint MINA LST upon cryptographic proof of a valid MINA lock event.
